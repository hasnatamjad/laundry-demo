What We Offer

At Garment Care Express, we pride ourselves on offering a reliable, dependable, high-quality laundry service at an affordable price. We provide everything from dry cleaning and duvet cleaning, to service washes, commercial laundry, shirt washing and ironing.

Our laundry service is designed to provide you with a simple, stress-free means of keeping your clothes and bedding clean and fresh. We run a self-service launderette in Brighton, as well as offering a comprehensive laundry service wash option. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff are on hand at the laundrette to help with all washes, while our service wash package includes the option to add free collection and delivery. If you’re short on time and you’ve got laundry to do, this is the perfect, hassle-free solution. We can collect your laundry, bring it to our launderette in Hove, wash, dry, and fold it with the utmost care, and then deliver it back to you.

We also offer expert duvet cleaning and dry cleaning, and we run a competitively-priced ironing service.
